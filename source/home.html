Variation of CDF Allocation
Variation of the CDF allocation across Components (Community Projects, Youth and Women Empowerment and Secondary school boarding and Skills Development Bursaries) shall not be allowed unless with express permission by the Minister.
Administrative Cost
The administrative cost related to the management, disbursement, utilisation and accountability of the CDF shall not exceed 5% of the total Fund.

OTHER SERVICES

Garbage Collection

Waste collection is a part of the process of waste management. It is the transfer of solid waste from the point of use and disposal to the point of treatment or landfill.

Dumpster Renting

With a dumpster rental, you choose what size dumpster you need and your service provider will drop it off at your desired location for you to fill as needed.

Portable Toilets

A portable or mobile toilet is any type of toilet that can be moved around, some by one person, some by mechanical equipment such as a truck and crane.

Zero Waste

Zero waste refers not only to keeping waste out of landfill, but also pushing our economy to be less wasteful in production and consumption.

Recycling

Recycling is the process of converting waste materials into new materials and objects.

Industry Pickup

Industrial truck, carrier designed to transport materials within a factory area with maximum flexibility in making moves.
